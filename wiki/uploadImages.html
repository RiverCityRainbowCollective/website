How to Upload Images

Uploading images to add to your pages is a little cumbersome, but nothing too complicated. This guide will walk you through the process.

 

  1. Select the Insert Assets button on the editor toolbar.

2. Select the wiki-assets folder to ensure we upload our assets there. We want to keep our asset files organized so they don't clutter up the wiki. If you've already uploaded assets in this session, you may be already in the wiki-assets folder.

3. Browse for files on your local system or Drag and Drop them into the box. This would also be a good place to create a new folder for your page to store the assets just for your page, to keep things even more organized. This is optional but recommended. 

4. After your upload is completed, you should see your asset file in the assets list. Select your asset them select INSERT.

Your asset should now be visible in your page!  You're done!
